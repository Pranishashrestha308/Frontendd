About Us

Kalinchowk Resort is a premier destination nestled in the breathtaking landscapes of the Himalayas. Our resort offers a unique blend of comfort, adventure, and cultural immersion. We are dedicated to providing an unforgettable experience for our guests, with warm hospitality and exceptional service.

Surrounded by stunning views of snow-capped mountains and lush valleys, Kalinchowk Resort is the perfect escape for those seeking tranquility and adventure. Whether you're a nature lover, a thrill-seeker, or simply looking to relax and rejuvenate, our resort offers something for everyone.

Activities

At Kalinchowk Resort, we offer a wide range of activities and amenities to make your stay truly memorable. Some of our offerings include:

  • Comfortable Accommodation
  • Delicious Local and International Cuisine
  • Trekking and Hiking
  • Visit to Kalinchowk Bhagwati Temple
  • Cultural Experiences
  • Stunning Sunrise and Sunset Views
